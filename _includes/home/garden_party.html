Koii is a framework for the new Internet

The Narcissus Flower is the premier unveiling of the Dynamic NFT standard, which creates an Internet made for and by the people. It rewards people for using the Internet.

When people get rewarded, it creates an Internet that is more rewarding for everyone, where data is owned by its creator and all creators are paid for their work.

Dynamic NFTs are one example of the Atomic NFT standard, the fundamental building block of the fair, community-owned Internet.
