How does it work?

Layer N

  • Write to Koii nodes instead of directly on chain
  • Applications are cross-chain by default
  • Users can easily join without buying tokens first (easy onboarding)

Gradual Consensus

  • Proof of Stake games are defined by three functions (“Tasks”)
  • Highly scalable: Gas fees are much cheaper than traditional hosting costs - most interactions are feeless
  • Eco-friendly
  • Designed for humans, not finance

Proof of Real Traffic

  • Track verified human attention on any content
  • Fairly reward users based on traffic
  • Prevent spam and collusion to monopolize rewards
